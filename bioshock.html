Bioshock 1 & 2

  • Description: Bioshock 1 and 2 are immersive first-person shooters set in the atmospheric and dystopian underwater city of Rapture. The games seamlessly blend intense gunplay with supernatural abilities granted by genetic modifications known as Plasmids. Players navigate a decaying and art-deco-inspired world, facing off against genetically altered enemies and iconic foes like the formidable Big Daddies. The narrative-driven gameplay, moral choices, and unique setting contribute to the enduring appeal of the Bioshock series


  • Genre: First-Person Shooter, Horror
  • Duration:12 to 15 hours
  • Immersive storytelling and atmospheric ambiance
  • Exploration of philosophical themes and ethical dilemmas
