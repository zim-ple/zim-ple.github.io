Little Nightmares

  • Description: the journey of Six, a small, raincoat-clad girl, as she explores the mysterious and unsettling vessel known as The Maw. As players guide Six through the dark and atmospheric environment, they encounter grotesque and enigmatic creatures, navigating through puzzles and using stealth to survive. The narrative unfolds with a sense of ambiguity, inviting players to interpret the story's themes of fear and helplessness in this haunting and visually distinctive world.


  • Genre: Third-Person, Psychological Horror
  • Short Duration: 4-6 hours
  • Puzzle-Platformer
  • Ambiguous Narrative
  • Dark Atmosphere
  • Suspenseful Gameplay
  • Stealth mechanics
