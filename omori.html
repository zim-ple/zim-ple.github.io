OMORI

  • Description: Omori, a mysterious boy with a penchant for dreams and a surreal world within his mind. The game explores themes of friendship, trauma, and self-discovery as Omori navigates between the real world and the dreamlike "White Space." Filled with emotional depth and unique storytelling, "Omori" offers a journey through the complexities of the human mind and the hauntingly beautiful landscapes of both reality and imagination.


  • Genre: Role-playing game (RPG), psychological horror, adventure
  • Duration:20 to 30 hours
  • Turn-based combat system
  • Puzzle-solving and exploration
  • Atmospheric Sound Design
  • Distinctive hand-drawn art style
